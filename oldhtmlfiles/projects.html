December Rose Photography

December Rose Photography

Web Design, Logo Design


December Rose Photography is a photography business based out of Buckley, WA. DRP needed a logo designed to be used as a watermark for photos and also a company logo. After discussions with the client, the decision was made to use a semi-photo-realistic design which visually communicates the idea behind the studio's name; a rose in December is both treasured and something to be remembered, just like a great photograph.

Franks Motor Bikes

Frank's Motor Bikes

Web Design


Franks Motor Bikes is a motorcycle dealer located in Bellevue, WA. They specialize in somewhat uncommon Sherco Motorcycles. Because of the specialized scope of the business, Frank needed a site to promote the business and give it exposure on the net since a lot of the customer base is generated online. The site was designed to accomplish that goal and provide easy access to information on Sherco Motorcycles, who Frank's Motor Bikes is, and what they have to offer.




Skills

Photoshop, Illustrator, HTML, CSS, PHP, Flash
